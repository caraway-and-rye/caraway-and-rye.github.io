# Soy Tare
Used to flavor broth for ramen.

### Ingredients:
* 1/2 cup soy sauce
* 2 tbsp mirin
* 1 tbsp sake (or dry sherry)
* 1 tsp sugar
* 1 tsp sesame oil
* 2 cloves garlic, grated
* 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
* 1 tsp fish sauce

### Serve with:
* broth
* spring onions
* choice of meat
* noodles


### How to make:

Gently warm everything together in a small pot (do not boil), stirring until sugar dissolves. Strain out the garlic and ginger. Use 2 tbsp in each bowl of ramen.
