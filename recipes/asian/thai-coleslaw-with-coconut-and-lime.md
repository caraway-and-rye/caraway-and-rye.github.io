# Thai Coleslaw with Coconut and Lime

### Makes:

as much as you need

### Ingredients:

#### Coleslaw base 

* cabbage, sliced thinly
* carrot, grated
* under-ripe mango, cut into matchsticks


#### Flavoring extras

* green onions, sliced diagonally
* lime zest
* ginger, grated
* cilantro leaves, roughly chopped
* mint, roughly chopped
* birds eye chilli, thinly sliced


#### Dressing

* cocnut milk (ideally scoop the fatty clumps)
* fish sauce
* lime juice
* brown sugar
* salt, if needed
* thai curry paste, 


#### Toppings

* toasted shredded coconut
* peanuts, roughly chopped


### Timing:
Preparation time: 30 minutes

### How to Make:

1. Chop and place in a bowl as many of the base and flavoring ingredients. Enough for the next meal.

2. Add dressing ingredients to taste and mix well. Let the coleslaw sit a bit for the flavors to combine. Especially the sugar needs a little time to melt and come through.

3. Sprinkle any toppings on when serving.

### Notes:

Leftover coconut milk can be frozen to use in a soup. It will become grainy, but will melt back to normal when heated.

Last time we didn't use any mango or curry paste. No toppings either.
