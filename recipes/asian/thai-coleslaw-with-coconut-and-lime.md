# Thai Coleslaw with Coconut and Lime

### Makes:
2, 4, or 6 servings

### Ingredients:

#### Dressing
| Ingredient | 2 servings | 4 servings | 6 servings |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Coconut milk (light)** | 1/2 cup | 1 cup | 1 1/2 cups |
| **Lime juice and zest** | 1/2 lime | 1 lime | 1 1/2 limes |
| **Fish sauce** | 2 tsp | 4 tsp | 6 tsp |
| **Dark brown sugar** | 1 tsp | 2 tsp | 3 tsp |
| **Thai green curry paste** | 1/8 tsp | 1/4 tsp | 3/8 tsp |

#### Coleslaw
| Ingredient | 2 servings | 4 servings | 6 servings |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Green cabbage** | 2 cups | 4 cups | 6 cups |
| **Red cabbage** | 2 cups | 4 cups | 6 cups |
| **Mango** | 1/2 | 1 | 1 1/2 |
| **Fresh mint leaves** | 1/4 cup | 1/2 cup | 3/4 cup |
| **Cilantro leaves** | 1/4 cup | 1/2 cup | 3/4 cup |
| **Green onions** | 1 | 2 | 3 |
| **Toasted shredded coconut** | 1/4 cup | 1/2 cup | 3/4 cup |
| **Chopped peanuts** | 1/4 cup | 1/2 cup | 3/4 cup |

### Timing:
Preparation time: 30 minutes

### How to Make:
1. Blend the dressing ingredients in a blender until smooth. Taste and adjust seasonings if needed.

2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced cabbages, mango, mint, cilantro, and green onions. Toss to mix well.

3. Add about half the dressing and toss. Taste and add more dressing if desired.

4. Sprinkle toasted coconut and chopped peanuts on top before serving.

### Cook's Notes:
The "secret" is the Thai curry paste. If you can't find it, substitute 1-2 teaspoons of jalapeño and grated fresh ginger.
