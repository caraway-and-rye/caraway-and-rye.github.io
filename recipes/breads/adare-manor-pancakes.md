# Adare Manor Pancakes

### Before You Begin

Egg whites are weighed (~30 g per egg). Make the meringue first, then fold into the batter just before cooking.

### Serves:

Roughly 1, 2, 3, or 4 portions of pancakes

### Ingredients:

| Ingredient | 1 egg | 2 eggs | 3 eggs | 4 eggs |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Egg whites** | 30 g | 60 g | 90 g | 120 g |
| **Caster sugar** (for the meringue) | 2 g | 4 g | 6 g | 8 g |
| **Icing sugar** | 12 g | 25 g | 38 g | 50 g |
| **Baking powder** | 4 g | 8 g | 11 g | 15 g |
| **Plain flour** | 80 g | 160 g | 240 g | 320 g |
| **Whole milk** | 125 ml | 250 ml | 375 ml | 500 ml |
| **Vegetable oil** | 30 g | 60 g | 90 g | 120 g |

### Preparation:

1. Whisk the egg whites with the caster sugar to a soft-peak meringue.
2. Mix all the other ingredients together into a smooth batter.
3. Fold the meringue into the batter.
4. Cook on a buttered pan over medium heat until bubbles form, then flip.

### Notes:

Full batch (8 eggs / 240 g egg whites) uses 15 g caster sugar, 100 g icing sugar, 30 g baking powder, 640 g flour, 1 L milk, and 240 g vegetable oil.
