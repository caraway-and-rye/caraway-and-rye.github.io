# Adare Manor Pancakes

### For pancakes:
* 8g caster sugar
* 120g egg whites
* 50g icing sugar
* 15g baking powder
* 320g plain flour
* 500ml milk
* 120g vegetable oil

### To serve:
* fruit
* maple syrup
* butter

### How to make:

Make a meringue from the egg whites and caster sugar.

Mix all other ingredients together in a large bowl.

Integrate the meringue.
