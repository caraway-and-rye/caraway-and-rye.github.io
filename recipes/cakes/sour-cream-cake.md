# Sour-cream cake (with fruit)
## Chec cu smantana si fructe

![image](/recipes/images/sour-cream-cake.jpeg)

### Ingredients:
* 4 eggs
* 200 g caster sugar (+ extra if using a very sour liquid)
* 1/3 cup liquid (eg. sweet wine, sherry, port, milk, lemon juice, etc)
* 350 g sour cream (30-40%)
* vanilla
* *optional* additional flavoring (eg. lemon peel)
* 375 g flour
* 2 tsp baking powder (maybe add extra if the liquid used is very sour)
* *optional* 200 - 250g fruit, fresh or frozen + 1-2 spoons of starch to coat it (if using frozen fruit, don't defrost it; cut the fruit in chunks)

### Makes:

20 cm square cake

### How to make:

Measure the flour and mix with the baking powder. Heat oven to 160C fan / 180C.

Beat eggs, sugar and vanilla. Add liquid, cream, additional flavorings and a bit of flour. Beat a while longer to combine smoothly. 

Add all the flour and beat quickly to combine. Pour in a square pan and level. Arrange the fruit on top. Bake until done (40-50 mins).

### Variations:

#### sauternes wine and frozen raspberries

Took a little over 2 tbsp of potato starch to coat the frozen raspberries. Cake is sweet but not overly so; raspberries are tart. Took 45-50 minutes in the oven.
