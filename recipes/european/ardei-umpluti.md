# Ardei Umpluți (Stuffed Bell Peppers)

### Before You Begin
Prepare all ingredients. Traditional Romanian stuffed peppers require careful preparation and patience.

### Makes:
3, 5, or 8 servings

### Ingredients:

| Ingredient | 3 servings | 5 servings | 8 servings |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bell Peppers** | 5-6 medium | 10-12 medium | 16-18 medium |
| **Ground Meat** | 350g | 600g | 950g |
| **Rice** | 40ml | 75ml | 120ml |
| **Onions** | 1 | 2 | 3 |
| **Fresh Dill** | 1/4 bunch | 1/2 bunch | 3/4 bunch |
| **Fresh Parsley** | 1/4 bunch | 1/2 bunch | 3/4 bunch |
| **Ground Black Pepper** | 1/2 tsp | 1 tsp | 1 1/2 tsp |
| **Salt** | 1/2 tsp | 1 tsp | 1 1/2 tsp |
| **Sweet Paprika** | 1/2 tsp | 1 tsp | 1 1/2 tsp |
| **Bay Leaves** | 1-2 | 3 | 4-5 |

### Sauce Ingredients:
| Ingredient | 3 servings | 5 servings | 8 servings |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tomato Concentrate** | 125ml | 250ml | 400ml |
| **Olive Oil** | 1 1/2 tbsp | 3 tbsp | 4 1/2 tbsp |
| **Flour** | 1 tbsp | 2 tbsp | 3 tbsp |

### How to Make:
1. Finely chop onions and herbs. Mix with ground meat, rice, salt, pepper, and paprika.
2. Core bell peppers and carefully fill with meat mixture.
3. Arrange stuffed peppers in a deep cooking pot.
4. Prepare sauce by mixing tomato concentrate, oil, flour, and seasonings.
5. Pour sauce over peppers, add bay leaves.
6. Simmer on low heat for 1 hour until peppers are tender and meat is cooked through.

### Timing:
- Preparation: 20 minutes
- Cooking: 1 hour
- Total time: 1 hour 20 minutes
