# Beef Stew
### Before You Begin
Preheat oven to 160°C. Put the kettle on to boil water.

### Makes:
3, 5, or 8 servings

### Ingredients:

| Ingredient | 3 servings | 5 servings | 8 servings |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Celery sticks** | 1 | 2 | 3 |
| **Onion** | 1/2 | 1 | 1-2 |
| **Carrots** | 1 | 2 | 3 |
| **Bay leaves** | 3 | 5 | 8 |
| **Thyme sprigs** | 1 | 2 | 3 |
| **Vegetable oil** | 2 tsp | 1 tbsp | 1 1/2 tbsp |
| **Butter** | 2 tsp | 1 tbsp | 1 1/2 tbsp |
| **Plain flour** | 1 tbsp | 2 tbsp | 3 tbsp |
| **Tomato purée** | 1 tbsp | 2 tbsp | 3 tbsp |
| **Worcestershire sauce** | 1 tbsp | 2 tbsp | 3 tbsp |
| **Beef stock cubes** | 1 | 2 | 3 |
| **Stewing beef** | 510g | 850g | 1.3kg |

### How to Make:
1. In a flameproof casserole dish, combine vegetables, oil, and butter. Cook over medium heat until softened, about 10 minutes.

2. Stir in flour until no longer dusty. Add tomato purée, Worcestershire sauce, and crumbled stock cubes.

3. Gradually pour in hot water, then add beef chunks. Bring to a gentle simmer.

4. Cover and bake in the oven for 2½ hours. Uncover and continue cooking for 30-60 minutes until meat is very tender and sauce has thickened.

5. Garnish with fresh thyme leaves before serving.

### Timing:
Total time: About 4 hours
- Preparation: 15 minutes
- Cooking: 3 hours 50 minutes
