# Chicken Tikka Masala

### Before You Begin
Gather all ingredients and ensure your chicken is cubed.

### Makes:
4, 6, or 10 servings

### Ingredients:

| Ingredient | 4 servings | 6 servings | 10 servings |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vegetable oil** | 2 tbsp | 3 tbsp | 5 tbsp |
| **Butter** | 12g | 18g | 30g |
| **Onions** | 2, roughly chopped | 3, roughly chopped | 5, roughly chopped |
| **Tikka masala paste** | 3 tbsp | 4-5 tbsp | 7-8 tbsp |
| **Red peppers** | 1, deseeded and chopped | 1-2, deseeded and chopped | 2-3, deseeded and chopped |
| **Chicken breasts** | 4, cubed | 6, cubed | 10, cubed |
| **Chopped tomatoes** | 1 x 400g can | 1.5 x 400g cans | 2.5 x 400g cans |
| **Tomato purée** | 2 tbsp | 3 tbsp | 5 tbsp |
| **Mango chutney** | 1-2 tbsp | 2-3 tbsp | 3-4 tbsp |
| **Double cream** | 75ml | 110ml | 180ml |
| **Natural yogurt** | 75ml | 110ml | 180ml |
| **Coriander leaves** | For garnish | For garnish | For garnish |

### How to Make
1. In a large casserole, heat oil and butter. Add onions with a pinch of salt and cook until soft and golden, about 15-20 minutes.

2. Stir in tikka masala paste and red peppers. Cook for 5 minutes to soften the peppers and release spice flavors.

3. Add chicken and coat thoroughly with the spice mixture. Cook for 2 minutes, then add chopped tomatoes, tomato purée, and water. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes until chicken is cooked through.

4. Remove lid and stir in mango chutney, cream, and yogurt. Warm gently and season to taste.

5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve with basmati rice and naan bread.

### Timing:
Preparation: 15 minutes
Cooking: 50 minutes
Total time: 1 hour 5 minutes

### Storage
Can be frozen for up to three months in an airtight container.
