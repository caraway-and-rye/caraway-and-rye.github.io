# Easy Classic Lasagne
### Before You Begin
Prepare all ingredients. You'll need a large saucepan and a roasting tin or casserole dish.

### Makes:
3, 5, or 8 servings

### Ingredients:

| Ingredient | 3 servings | 5 servings | 8 servings |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Olive oil** | 1/2 tbsp | 1 tbsp | 1 1/2 tbsp |
| **Smoked streaky bacon** | 1 rasher | 2 rashers | 3 rashers |
| **Onion** | 1/2 | 1 | 1 1/2 |
| **Celery stick** | 1/2 | 1 | 1 1/2 |
| **Carrot** | 1/2 medium | 1 medium | 1 1/2 medium |
| **Garlic cloves** | 1 | 2 | 3 |
| **Beef mince** | 250g | 500g | 750g |
| **Tomato purée** | 1/2 tbsp | 1 tbsp | 1 1/2 tbsp |
| **Chopped tomatoes** | 1 x 400g can | 2 x 400g cans | 3 x 400g cans |
| **Clear honey** | 1/2 tbsp | 1 tbsp | 1 1/2 tbsp |
| **Fresh egg lasagne sheets** | 250g | 500g | 750g |
| **Crème fraîche** | 200ml | 400ml | 600ml |
| **Mozzarella** | 50g | 125g | 190g |
| **Parmesan** | 25g | 50g | 75g |
| **Basil leaves** | Small handful | Large handful | 2 large handfuls |

### How to Make:
1. In a large saucepan, cook chopped bacon until it starts to turn golden. Add onion, celery, and carrot, cooking until softened.

2. Add garlic and cook briefly. Add beef mince, breaking it up and cooking until browned.

3. Stir in tomato purée, then add chopped tomatoes. Rinse tomato cans with water and add to the pan. Add honey and season. Simmer for 20 minutes.

4. Preheat oven to 200°C/180°C fan/gas 6. Layer sauce and lasagne sheets in a baking dish, finishing with a pasta layer.

5. Mix crème fraîche with a little water to create a pourable sauce. Pour over the top, then sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan.

6. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden and bubbling. Garnish with torn basil leaves if desired.

### Timing:
Total time: About 1 hour 15 minutes
Prep time: 15 minutes
Cook time: 1 hour
