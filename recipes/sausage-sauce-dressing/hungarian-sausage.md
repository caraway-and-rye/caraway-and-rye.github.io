# Hungarian Sausage

![image](/recipes/images/hungarian-sausage.jpg)

### Ingredients:

* 1 kg ground pork and beef (5:2)
* 1/2 cup cold beer
* 1/2 tbsp salt

#### Spices to grind:

* 1 tsp whole anise seeds
* 1 tsp whole mustard seeds
* 1 tbsp black pepper
* 3-4 bay leaves, torn into pieces
* 1 tsp dried coriander

#### Other spices:

* 1 tbsp mild paprika
* 1 tbsp hot paprika
* 3-4 cloves, minced
* 1 tsp brown sugar
* 1 tsp ground thyme


### How to make:

Grind the spices. Knead everything until very well combined; can also use a mixer. Use the same day or weigh 100-150 g servings and freeze.
