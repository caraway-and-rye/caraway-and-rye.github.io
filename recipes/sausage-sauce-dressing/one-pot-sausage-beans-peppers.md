# One-pot Sausage, Beans & Peppers
### Before You Begin
Get all ingredients ready. This is a quick one-pot meal that comes together fast.

### Makes:
2, 4, or 6 servings

### Ingredients:

| Ingredient | 2 servings | 4 servings | 6 servings |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Olive oil** | 1 tbsp | 2 tbsp | 3 tbsp |
| **Onion, finely chopped** | 1/2 large | 1 large | 1 1/2 large |
| **Red pepper, deseeded and sliced** | 1/2 | 1 | 1 1/2 |
| **Yellow pepper, deseeded and sliced** | 1/2 | 1 | 1 1/2 |
| **Sausages, cut into pieces** | 200g | 400g | 600g |
| **Cannellini beans, canned** | 200g | 400g | 600g |
| **Borlotti beans, canned** | 200g | 400g | 600g |
| **Rose harissa** | 1 tbsp | 2 tbsp | 3 tbsp |
| **Garlic granules** | 1/2 tbsp | 1 tbsp | 1 1/2 tbsp |
| **Sugar** | 1/2 tsp | 1 heaped tsp | 1 1/2 heaped tsp |
| **Crusty bread** | to serve | to serve | to serve |

### How to Make:
1. Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Saut√© onions until translucent and lightly browned, about 3-4 minutes.

2. Add peppers and cook until softening, around 4-5 minutes. Stir in sausage pieces and cook until lightly browned all over.

3. Add beans with their liquid, harissa, garlic granules, sugar, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 10-15 minutes until slightly thickened and sausages are fully cooked.

4. Serve hot with crusty bread.

### Timing:
- Prep Time: 5 minutes
- Cook Time: 30 minutes
- Total Time: 35 minutes
